# How to Find "Authorized Domains" in Firebase Console

## Step-by-Step Navigation

### Current Location
You're currently on: **Authentication** → **Users** tab

### Where to Go

1. **Click the "Settings" tab**
   - Look at the top of the Authentication page
   - You'll see tabs: "Users" | "Sign-in method" | "Templates" | "Usage" | **"Settings"** | "Extensions"
   - Click on **"Settings"**

2. **Scroll Down**
   - Once you're on the Settings tab, scroll down the page
   - Look for a section called **"Authorized domains"**

3. **You'll See**
   - A list of authorized domains (usually includes `localhost` and your Firebase project domain)
   - An **"Add domain"** button
   - Click **"Add domain"** to add `gouache.art`

## Visual Guide

```
Firebase Console
└── Authentication (left sidebar)
    └── Settings tab (top navigation)
        └── Scroll down
            └── "Authorized domains" section
                └── "Add domain" button
```

## Quick Path

**Authentication** → **Settings tab** → **Scroll down** → **"Authorized domains"**
